## Implementation Choices Analysis

### [Spec] Centralized color/theme management
| Ticket | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| AIB-359 | `participant-colors.ts` — utility functions returning color objects with 5 properties (text, border, bgSubtle, bgMedium, stroke) |
| AIB-360 | `comparison-theme.ts` — full `ComparisonAccentTheme` interface with 12+ properties, derived named themes (winnerTheme, runnerUpTheme), context-aware helpers |

**Best**: AIB-360 — Defines a typed interface, provides context-specific helpers (compliance, verdict, stat), and centralizes all color decisions in one file.

### [Spec] Component testability
| Ticket | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| AIB-359 | No new data-testid attributes added |
| AIB-360 | Comprehensive data-testid on decision cards, verdict badges, recommendation, legend, stat cards, progress bars |

**Best**: AIB-360 — Test-driven approach with systematic testability improvements.

| Ticket | Approach | Constitution | Trade-offs |
|--------|----------|--------------|------------|
| AIB-359 | Removes Card from some sections, replaces with custom divs | Partial — diverges from shadcn pattern | Lighter DOM, but inconsistent |

**Best**: AIB-360 — Composes shadcn/ui Card rather than replacing it, aligning with Constitution Principle II.

### Decision Point 2: Theme Extensibility
| Ticket | Approach | Constitution | Trade-offs |

**Best**: AIB-360 — Theme interface prevents inconsistency across components; helpers prevent repetitive theme lookups.

### Decision Point 3: Test Coverage
